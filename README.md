# Nova Paw Festival 2026

The Nova Paw Festival is Qatar’s first international pet festival, taking place on November 27th & 28th, 2026 at the iconic pet park The Pearl-Qatar.

Bringing together over 6,000 visitors across two unforgettable days, the festival is designed as a celebration for pet lovers, families, breeders, trainers, and animal enthusiasts from across the region and beyond.

What makes Nova Paw Festival truly unique is its internationally recognized judging panel. All dog competitions are judged by officials from the World Kennel Union (WKU), while cat competitions are evaluated by judges from the World Cat Federation (WCF), making this one of the first events of its kind in Qatar to host globally sanctioned pet competitions.

The festival offers a full experience filled with entertainment, education, and community engagement. Visitors can enjoy exciting dog and cat competitions including fashion shows, grooming contests, best-in-show championships, and special showcase performances.

Beyond the competitions, guests can explore adoption zones, free veterinary consultations, educational vet talks, family-friendly activities, live entertainment, carnival experiences, food trucks, shopping booths, and interactive spaces designed for both pets and their owners.

Nova Paw Festival is more than just an event, it is a destination where passion for pets, community connection, and world-class entertainment come together to create an unforgettable experience for everyone.

## 🏆 Competition Registration Requirements

### 1. Grooming Competition
- **Categories:**
  - Session 1 (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM): Dog Figure Grooming (Artificial)
  - Session 2 (6:30 PM - 8:30 PM): Real Dog Grooming
- **Required Fields:** Full Name, Mobile, Email, Address, Experience Level, Dog Name/Breed/Age, Pet Passport, Vaccination Record.
- **Rules:** Health check mandatory (Nova Vet if not available), vaccination up to date.

### 2. Dog Fashion Show
- **Required Fields:** Owner Name, Mobile, Email, Dog Name/Breed/Age, Pet Passport.
- **Rules:** Matching Owner/Pet outfits required, costumes must respect local culture.

### 3. Cat Fashion Show
- **Required Fields:** Owner Name, Mobile, Email, Address, Cat Name/Breed/Age, Pet Passport.
- **Rules:** Matching Owner/Pet outfits, cats must remain in carriers except during runway.

### 4. Best Looking Dog Show
- **Required Fields:** Owner Name, Mobile, Email, Dog Name/Breed/Age, Pet Passport.
- **Rules:** Fully vaccinated, non-aggressive, fit for participation.

### 5. Best Looking Cat Show
- **Required Fields:** Owner Name, Mobile, Email, Cat Name/Breed/Age, Pet Passport.
- **Rules:** Fully vaccinated, healthy, must remain in carrier except during judging.

### 6. Drawing Cat Battle
- **Required Fields:** Name, Mobile, Email, Age, Experience Level, Materials List.
- **Rules:** Participants bring own equipment, 1-hour duration, artwork must follow decency standards.

## 🎟️ The Unified User Journey

Nova Paw Festival uses a high-fidelity, unified dashboard system to manage the visitor experience.

### Phase 1: Secure Your Entry (Tickets)
- **High-Fidelity Checkout:** A premium 5-step wizard with real-time running totals and consistent "Back" navigation.
- **Smart Flows:** Dynamic logic for Adults, Kids, Dog Owners, and Cat Owners.
- **Safety Compliance:** Mandatory safety agreements integrated directly into the purchase flow.
- **Outcome:** Instant QR code generation and access to the personal Dashboard.

### Phase 2: Manage & Participate (Dashboard)
- **Digital Ticket:** High-fidelity ticket view with QR code, order breakdown, and venue details.
- **Dynamic Invitations:** The dashboard intelligently offers competition entries based on your ticket tier (e.g., Dog Owners see Grooming contests).
- **Session Sync:** Basic info (Name, Phone, Email) is saved and automatically synced across all festival flows.

### Phase 3: The Spotlight (Competitions)
- **Elite Registration:** A specialized 7-step flow for international competitions (WKU/WCF).
- **Health Compliance:** Integrated file uploads for mandatory Pet Passports and Vaccination Records.
- **Expert Review:** All applications are held in a "Pending" state for official judge review.

## 🛠️ Design System (The "7-Step Wizard" Pattern)
All interactive flows in Nova Paw Festival follow a standardized, premium design pattern:
- **Layout Shell:** Persistent step indicators with monochromatic paths and primary accents.
- **Typography:** Ultra-bold `font-display` headers and `9px` uppercase metadata labels.
- **Micro-Interactions:** State preservation, auto-filling, and non-reloading transitions.
- **Visual Style:** Minimalist "Sharp" aesthetic (`rounded-sm`) with a Bone-white (`#F5F5F0`) palette.
